Coding Quiz Challenge

This is not really a challenge. If you really want to know the answers, just CNTRL-SHIFT-J and look into the script!
However, if you want to play, this is how it works:

  1. The quiz is timed (60 seconds).
  2. Your score is the amount of time you have left (higher is 'better')
  3. For incorrect answers you will be deducted 10 seconds from your remaining time (and hence score)
  4. If you're lucky, or know me well, you might get onto the high-scorers list! (or you can just modify the localStorage items...)
